# MC Dungeons Weapons & Armors — Tweaks

## Recipe Changes (via KubeJS)

- **Remove ALL crafting recipes** from both Weapons and Armors mods — MC Dungeons equipment cannot be crafted
- These are exploration-only finds

## Mob Equipment (via KubeJS / custom mod)

- Zombies and skeletons have a **1/20 chance** to spawn wearing/holding random MC Dungeons weapons and/or armor
- This is the only way to obtain MC Dungeons gear — kill mobs and take it
- Stacks with Surface Safety mod's Y-level gear scaling (iron armor + dungeons weapon/armor possible on deep mobs)

## Intent

MC Dungeons gear is rare loot drops, not craftable progression items. Finding a skeleton with unique armor and a glaive is exciting because you can't just make them.
