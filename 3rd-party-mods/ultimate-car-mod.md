# Ultimate Car Mod — Tweaks

## Recipe Modifications (via KubeJS)

### Car Body
- Replace **colored concrete** with **IE colored sheetmetal** for all 16 car body colors
- Red car = red sheetmetal, blue car = blue sheetmetal, etc.

### Fuel Chain
- Replace the mod's canola → methanol → bio diesel chain with **Immersive Petroleum diesel**
- Disable or remove canola farming / canola press recipes
- Gas stations should accept IE Petroleum's diesel

### Frames
- **Wooden car variants** use **IE treated wood** for the frame
- **Metal car variants** use **IE steel** for the frame

### Assembly
- **Remove Car Workshop** multiblock entirely
- Car components crafted via **Create Sequenced Assembly** and **Mechanical Crafting**
- Final car assembly via **Mechanical Crafting**
- Use **Create stash** instead of vanilla chest in any storage-related recipes

### Wheels (Sequenced Assembly)
- Iron Sheet (Create press) → Spout bitumen (IP refining byproduct) → Press → **Wheel**
- Big Wheels: same process, more bitumen
- Ties wheel production to Immersive Petroleum oil refining

### Engines (Sequenced Assembly)
- Replace iron block + piston + flint recipe with a Sequenced Assembly line using **steel** components

### Tanks
- Replace mod's fuel tanks with **Create Fluid Tanks**

### Asphalt
- Keep UC's asphalt blocks, slopes, and road markings (more variety than IP's)
- Tweak asphalt recipe to use **IP bitumen** as the source material

### Fuel Chain
- **Remove entirely** — canola crop, oil mill, backmix reactor, split tank, mod's blast furnace, dynamo, generator, crank
- Cars use **Immersive Petroleum diesel**
- Gas Station block kept, accepts IP diesel — fuels both cars and planes

### Infrastructure Recipe Changes
- **License Plate** → requires **IE aluminum plates**
- **Gas Station** → requires **Create components + IE aluminum**
- **Guard Rails** → requires **IE aluminum**
- **Traffic Lights** → requires **IE steel**
- **Traffic Signs** → requires **iron**
- **Street Lights** → keep or tweak TBD
- **Car Painter** → keep as-is (2 dye + 2 sticks)
- Remove mod's fluid pipes/extractor (use Create)
